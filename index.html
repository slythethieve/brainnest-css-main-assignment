METAL GEAR SOLID

Metal Gear is a series of techno-thriller stealth games created by Hideo Kojima. Developed and published by Konami. The player often takes control of a special forces operative (usually Solid Snake or Big Boss), who is assigned the task of finding the titular superweapon, "Metal Gear", a bipedal walking tank with the ability to launch nuclear weapons.
